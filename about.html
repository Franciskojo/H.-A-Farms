Our Mission

At H. A. Farms, we are committed to providing fresh, organic, and sustainably grown produce while nurturing the land and supporting our local community. Every day, we strive to deliver farm-to-table goodness with integrity, transparency, and a deep respect for nature.

About H.A. Farms

H. A. Farms is a thriving agricultural enterprise based in Mankesim, Central Region, Ghana, strategically positioned at New Kumasi (fosu Station). Committed to excellence in poultry farming, the company focuses on the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality, healthy, and affordable poultry products, including: Premium Eggs – Fresh, nutrient-rich eggs produced under strict hygienic conditions. Quality Chicken – Healthy, well-raised poultry, free from harmful additives, ensuring superior taste and nutritional value.

Our Farm

Welcome to H. A. Farms, where nature, care, and quality come together to create food you can trust. Nestled in the heart of Ghana’s fertile lands, our farm is a thriving hub of sustainable agriculture. We raise our poultry and produce with integrity, combining traditional farming wisdom with modern best practices to deliver clean, nutritious, and ethically grown food.


What Makes Our Farm Special


Healthy, Free-Roaming Poultry : Our chickens are raised in spacious, stress-free environments with access to natural feed and clean water.
Daily-Fresh Eggs : Collected every morning, our eggs are known for their rich yolks, strong shells, and unbeatable taste.
Clean & Sustainable Practices : We use eco-friendly methods to reduce waste, enrich the soil, and protect our environment.
Passionate Farmers, Real Results : Our dedicated team works around the clock to ensure every product meets the highest standards of safety and quality.
Whether you're enjoying a hearty meal or planting the next season’s crops, H. A. Farms is where it all begins with honesty, heart, and a deep respect for the land. From our farm to your family, the freshness speaks for itself.
