Who We Are

About H.A. Farmsgh

H. A. Farmsgh is a thriving agricultural enterprise based in Mankesim, Central Region, Ghana, strategically positioned at New Kumasi (Fosu Station). Committed to excellence in poultry farming, we focus on the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality, healthy, and affordable poultry products.

Our range includes Premium Eggs – fresh, nutrient-rich eggs produced under strict hygienic conditions, and Quality Chicken – healthy, well-raised poultry, free from harmful additives, ensuring superior taste and nutritional value.
