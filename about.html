Our Mission

At Farm Fresh Organics, we are committed to providing fresh, organic, and sustainably grown produce while nurturing the land and supporting our local community. Every day, we strive to deliver farm-to-table goodness with integrity, transparency, and a deep respect for nature.

About H.A. Farms

H. A. Farms is a thriving agricultural enterprise based in Mankesim, Central Region, Ghana, strategically positioned at New Kumasi (Sofu Station). Committed to excellence in poultry farming, the company focuses on the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality, healthy, and affordable poultry products, including: Premium Eggs – Fresh, nutrient-rich eggs produced under strict hygienic conditions. Quality Chicken – Healthy, well-raised poultry, free from harmful additives, ensuring superior taste and nutritional value.

Our Farm

Located in the heart of Willamette Valley, our farm spans 50 acres of fertile soil where we grow seasonal vegetables, fruits, and herbs. We take pride in regenerative farming, no synthetic pesticides, and ethical labor practices.
